<h2 id="datalinker">Farming Operation</h2>
<p>The Farm Data Standards and DataLinker were developed as separate initiatives to help agricultural data be more accessible for 
farmers and to be used more effectively by solution and service providers.</p>

| Common name  | Definition | Source |
| ------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| Farm name | 1. A farm name is the name a farm is known by. It might be the farm owner's own personal name, or it might be something entirely different and unique to the farm. <br> 2. Name of the block the application is to be applied to. | [View](https://github.com/Datalinker-Org/Farm-Data-Standards/blob/master/Land%20Application%20Standard/LADS_Land-Applications-Data-Dictionary.md){:target="_blank"} |
| Farm Infrastructure | Infrastructure includes the farms, markets, and businesses that support the farms within a given area.  | [View](https://extension.psu.edu/agriculture-and-infrastructure-whats-the-connection){:target="_blank"} |
| Total area | Complete land area of an operating holding or other feature, regardless of land use or internal subdivision. | [View](https://www.farmax.co.nz/story/area-definitions-and-editing){:target="_blank"} |
| Leased area | An area of an operating farm holding that is not owned by the operating business entity or another entity within the business group. | - |
